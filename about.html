About This Project

This project helps people travelling to China see if they can benefit from Chinese visa exemption policies. These policies allow you to stay in China for a duration without having to apply a Chinese visa beforehand and without having to do extensive paperwork/contact/documents.

This project also aims to keep everything transparent. In addition to the assumptions stated below, there will be annotations and links leading to external/official sources; content I put here are often paraphrased, so it is always recommend that you look at the sources for yourselves to check for subtleties and other legal details to avoid confusion.

This project is dedicated to mainland China and as such will not be covering any visa exemption policies for entering Hongkong, Macau, or Taiwan.

Lastly, this is a personal project, and as such, any and all feedback/suggestions are appreciated. If there's anything bothering you or you'd like to see implemented, please reach out here!

Assumption: Durations in Months

Covention denotes that 1 month corresponds to 30 days, a pattern present in many Chinese publications. By extension, 3 months correspond to 90 days, 6 months to 180 days, etc. For consistency in policy durations, all measurements of "months" have been converted to their equivalent in "days".

Link Colors

Links in blue or green refer to external sources. Green-colored links are in Chinese, and blue-colored links are in English.
